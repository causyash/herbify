You should first create these distinct Categories in the Admin Dashboard:

4. **Natural Supplements** (Herbal capsules, tinctures, and drops)
5. **Skincare & Topicals** (Organic creams, salves, and serums)
6. **Superfoods & Powders** (Adaptogen powders and nutrient-dense plant bases)
