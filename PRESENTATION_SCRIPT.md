# 🌿 Herbify: Project Presentation Script (Clear & Simple Version)

This script is written for clarity and ease of understanding. It focuses on how the project works and what it achieves, without using overly complex jargon.

---

## 👥 The Project Team
1. **Sarang Mansi** - Team Lead (Opening & Closing)
2. **Patel Riya** - Backend & Database (Security & Logic)
3. **Prajapati Harshil** - Frontend & UI (User Experience)
4. **Patel Yash** - Admin & Integrations (Dashboard & Alerts)

---

## 🎙️ Part 1: The Introduction
**Speaker: Sarang Mansi**

*   **Mansi:** "Good morning everyone. I’m Sarang Mansi, and today we’re presenting **Herbify**—an e-commerce platform built for high-quality herbal products. Our team includes **Patel Riya**, **Patel Yash**, and **Prajapati Harshil**."
*   **Mansi:** "Our objective was simple: to create a secure, easy-to-use website where people can learn about medicinal herbs and buy natural products. The frontend is currently live on **Vercel**, and the backend is running on **Render**."
*   **Mansi:** "Harshil will now start by showing you how a user experiences the site."

---

## 🎙️ Part 2: Browsing & User Experience
**Speaker: Prajapati Harshil**
*(Action: Pilot scrolls slowly through the Home page and clicks on 'Herbs')*

*   **Harshil:** "As we scroll through the **Home Page**, you’ll see our featured collections. We focused on making the design clean and responsive so it works perfectly on mobile phones and desktops."
*   **Harshil:** "We have two main sections: **Herbs** and **Products**. Let's look at the Herbs section. Here, users can search for plants and read about their medical benefits."
*   *(Action: Pilot clicks on a specific herb and then a product)*
*   **Harshil:** "Every product page shows clear details, prices, and high-quality images. The website is built with **React**, which makes navigating between these pages very fast and smooth."

---

## 🎙️ Part 3: Security & Data
**Speaker: Patel Riya**
*(Action: Pilot goes to the Login/Register page)*

*   **Riya:** "While we log in, I’ll explain how we keep user data safe. I’m Patel Riya, and I managed the backend logic."
*   **Riya:** "We use **MongoDB** to store our data and **JSON Web Tokens** to handle user sessions. This ensures that when a user logs in, their information is protected. We also encrypt all passwords before storing them in the database."
*   **Riya:** "Basically, the backend makes sure that only the right people can access their accounts or make changes to their orders."

---

## 🎙️ Part 4: Shopping & Cart
**Speaker: Prajapati Harshil**
*(Action: Pilot adds a product to the cart and opens the cart page)*

*   **Harshil:** "Now, back to the user's journey. When I click 'Add to Cart', the item is saved instantly. Users can go to the **Cart Page** to review their items, change quantities, or remove them before checkout."
*   **Harshil:** "We used **React Context API** to manage this, so the cart stays updated no matter which page you are browsing."

---

## 🎙️ Part 5: Managing the Store (Admin)
**Speaker: Patel Yash**
*(Action: Pilot logs in as Admin and opens the Admin Dashboard)*

*   **Yash:** "Every online store needs management. I’m Patel Yash, and I’ll show you the **Admin Dashboard**."
*   **Yash:** "This is where the owner can see everything. We’ve added **charts** to track sales over time. Admins can also manage inventory—they can add new herbs, update products, and see who is registered on the site."
*   **Yash:** "One more thing: we integrated a **Telegram Bot**. So, whenever a user buys something or sends a message, the admin gets an instant notification on their phone. This helps the business respond quickly to customers."

---

## 🎙️ Part 6: Summary & Conclusion
**Speaker: Sarang Mansi**

*   **Mansi:** "In conclusion, **Herbify** is a complete project that covers everything from database management to a professional user interface."
*   **Mansi:** "We learned how to connect the frontend and backend, how to process payments, and how to keep a website secure. It was a great team effort to bring this project to life."
*   **Mansi:** "Thank you for your time. We are now happy to answer any questions you may have."
